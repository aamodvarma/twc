The World Conference Abu Dhabi


A series of global conferences, exploring the inherent oneness of humanity and a universal call to work for the betterment of the world. People from all backgrounds, cultures, religions, and ages are warmly invited.

The program will be structured around five themes:

• Vision for humanity
• Distance traversed
• Building vibrant communities
• Educational endeavours and the Training Institute
• Contributing to social transformation.
